How to Write an Argumentative Essay
  • **Introduction:** Hook the reader and state your clear thesis.
  • **Body Paragraphs:** Present your key points with evidence and analysis. Each paragraph should focus on one point.
  • **Counterargument:** Acknowledge and refute an opposing viewpoint.
  • **Conclusion:** Summarize your points and restate your thesis in a new way.
